1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, such as your name, email address, and phone number.

In addition, to provide our core AI-powered Tajwid analysis and feedback, we collect and process audio recordings of your recitations when you use the practice features of the app.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for the following key purposes:

  • Provide and improve our AI Tajwid analysis, helping you refine your pronunciation.
  • Personalize your learning experience, including tracking practice streaks and progress.
  • Process premium subscriptions and payments securely.
  • Send important service notifications and streak reminders.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your personal data and profile information are stored securely using Google Firebase.

We implement industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information and audio recordings from unauthorized access, loss, or alteration.

4. Account Deletion

We respect your ownership of your data. You can delete your account and all associated personal data, progress, and recordings at any time directly through the Settings menu inside the Tajwid Coach app.

If you no longer have the app installed or would prefer to request deletion online, you can submit an account and data deletion request here: Request Account Deletion Online.

Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

5. Third-Party Services

To deliver a seamless experience, we partner with reliable third-party service providers who assist us with key functions:

  • Google Firebase / Cloud Run: Infrastructure, authentication, database storage, and AI processing backend.
  • RevenueCat: Secure processing and management of in-app subscriptions and purchases.
